Our Tasty Tale

Born and raised in New Orleans, our family has been concocting recipes for many generations. With roots in the Louisiana shrimping industry, we are not strangers to fresh seafood. Growing up in a full house with seven kids, food has sort of taken place as the eighth child.

In New Orleans, during lent season, our dad would always stop by the shack on 4th street on the Westbank to pick up sacks of live crawfish. The crawfish during that time were about .39 cents a pound. And that’s the beginning of their tasty tale.

    Our Menu

    We pride ourselves on providing the freshest seafood to our customers. We only choose to source our seafood from the best vendors. Tasty Tails keeps it simple and authentic. We fly in Gambinos bread from Louisiana, use gulf shrimp and dress our po-boys in classic New Orleans style.

    Our Dungeness Crab is flown in directly from the pacific northwest, it comes from boat to table within 3 days of harvest. The Little Neck Clams are flown in fresh from Florida. We don’t take any shortcuts to save money, we want the best quality seafood on your table. Our catfish is from Mississippi, while many other places source their catfish overseas, from China or Vietnam. During the Gulf shrimp season, we get fresh, never frozen shrimp. Our Blue Crabs are world renowned, harvested from the waters of Lake Ponchartrain and the surrounding waters. Our crawfish is from Louisiana during the season, and flown in live from the west coast during offseason. -Khanh Tran
